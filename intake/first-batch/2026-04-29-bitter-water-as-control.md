# Bitter Water As Control

Every law that restricts women's reproductive choices while protecting men's echoes the bitter water ordeal. Every double standard about sexual behavior runs this same program. It's the same misogyny, just with better branding and legal authority instead of priestly authority.

The Bible isn't pro-life or pro-choice. It's pro-male control over female reproduction, with God as the enforcement mechanism. The bitter water ordeal is divine endorsement of reproductive coercion, miscarriage as punishment, pregnancy as conditional privilege revocable by male suspicion.

The ritual exists because it works, not at determining guilt, but at controlling women through the threat of reproductive violence. It establishes that women's bodies, pregnancies, and reproductive autonomy are subject to male emotion and priestly authority.

When only wives drink the poison, it is not justice. It is control.
