# Cosmic Monarchy Fossilized

Kings wrote law codes. Kings demanded tribute, loyalty, absolute obedience. Kings punished dissent with death and rewarded compliance with favor. Kings sat on thrones above their subjects and required approach on bended knee.

Then someone looked up and put a bigger king in the sky.

The structure is identical. Cosmic monarchy is political monarchy with an infinity symbol attached. Heaven is a court. Prayer is petition. Worship is tribute. Sin is treason. Hell is the dungeon. The entire theological architecture maps onto the power structures of the civilizations that produced it: throne rooms and vassal treaties and imperial enforcement, scaled to eternity.

The telling detail: when human civilizations eventually developed democracy, the cosmic model didn't update. God remained a monarch. He remained on a throne. He remained unelected, unaccountable, and absolute. The political structures evolved. The theology fossilized.
